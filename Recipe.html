Grandma's Chocolate Cake

It's one of the simple recipe which can be done within minutes and that too without any stress..

Ingrediants:

  • Oats-2 cups
  • Almonds-15
  • Cashews-15
  • Rasins-20
  • Cocoa Powder-5 tablespoons
  • milk-2 glasses
  • water- As required
  • Butter paper
  • Baking pan or cup
  • Baking soda

Procedure:

  1. Take a pan and mix all the nuts with oats.
  2. Add cocoa powder.
  3. Take a whisker and eventually mix the batter by adding milk and water eventually, without forming any lumps.
  4. Add little bit of baking powder and pour the mixture into a pan . Remember to greese the pan with butter and later add an butter paper to it so that it doesn't stick.
  5. Now microwave it for 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Delicious Chocolate cake is ready..
  7. You can glaze it with Chocolate syrup which is completely optional.
Preparation timings
Cooking Time Prep Time Servings
